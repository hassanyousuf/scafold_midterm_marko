History: Halal-ink

Halal-ink has been introduced to our muslim brothers and sister as a “halal” was to leave a (temperary) mark on there skin for the special occasion. We feel that muslims should have the chance to have a tattoo that they think looks nice. What we do is make sure it isn’t permanent. Just like conventional henna, our Halal-ink is made with the same products but we use a fine point pen and new techneques to give a more tattoo like appeal.
